1. Introduction

Delta Bot (the "Bot") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Bot.


2. Information We Collect

Account IDs

  • The Bot collects and stores Discord account IDs to provide its services.

    Message content

  • The Bot read message content but we do not store any message content from users.

    3. How We Use Your Information

  • Blacklist Management: We use your account ID to check if you are blacklisted from using the Bot. This helps ensure a safe and secure environment for all users.
  • User Support: Your account ID may be used to help resolve any issues or support requests you may have.

    4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share your information with third parties, except as required by law.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

    5. Data Security

    We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

    6. Data Retention

    We retain your account ID for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

    7. Your Rights

    You have the right to:
  • Access: Request access to the information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate information we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information, subject to legal requirements.

    8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website or within the Bot. Your continued use of the Bot after any such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.

    9. Compliance with German Law

    We operate in compliance with German data protection laws. All personal data collected by Delta Bot is processed and stored in accordance with the applicable laws and regulations of Germany, including but not limited to the Bundesdatenschutzgesetz (BDSG) and the Datenschutz-Grundverordnung (DSGVO). Our commitment to adhering to these regulations ensures that your data is handled with the utmost care and security.
